Facilities

Main Bar

The Main Bar (Sports Bar) is the center of the club’s activities with a safe and friendly environment for everyone to enjoy.
The main bar is complimented with numerous large screens which screen live sports games creating a great place for people to relax in.
In the summer months you can enjoy the warmth from the balcony. The smoker’s area is popular with the smokers and non-smokers which then follow onto a BBQ area.

On Thursday, Friday and Saturday nights bar snacks and main meals can be ordered from the bar between 6pm and 8:30pm, We also have Sunday Brunch from 11am – 2pm.

Children are allowed in all areas of the main bar with the exception of the gaming area. There is a small area for children equipped with boards and some toys for them to enjoy.

Gaming

Our Gaming Lounge offers seven individual gaming machines, some of our machines also offer multi games.

All seven of our gaming machines are linked to a mini Jackpot which must be struck by $100 along with the maxi Jackpot which must be struck by $1000.

All members and visitors are welcome to play the Gaming Machines, all visitors must ensure that they are signed into the club prior to commencing on the gaming machines.

8-Ball Pool & Snooker

We offer our Sub-club members and visitors a friendly outlet where men and women can meet in comfortable surroundings to play social or competitive 8-ball. Non-members are welcome and can be signed in at the Sports Bar.

Our club night is Thursday evening starting at 7.15pm with names for Drawn Pairs in by 7pm.

Entry fee $3.00 weekly with the last Thursday of the month being $5.00 for our ‘Double-up’ night. Our Winners prizes are 2 large Meat Packs with 2 small Meat Packs for the Runners-ups. On Double-up night 2 extra large Meat Packs for the Winners with no runner up prizes given out. Tournaments are held throughout the year at our club and affiliated clubs for those members who wish to play competitively.

We also have a snooker room onsite which holds four snooker tables. The snooker club is active throughout the year. For further details about club nights or competition nights please contact us.

Peacockes

The Peacockes Restaurant is a newly redecorated and fully functional dine-in style restaurant.

You can choose to dine in the restaurant or eat in the main bar with the bar snack menu. Each week the kitchen has their own specials written on the specials board.

The Peacockes Restaurant is also available to hire for smaller functions with seating up to 60 people.

Hours:
Thurs - Sat: 6:00pm - 8:30pm, Sun: 11:00am-2:00pm

The Riverview Lounge

The Riverview Lounge is a large lounge with its own bar facilities. The Riverview has held functions from birthdays, work functions, presentations, conferences, weddings, family functions and much more.

The Riverview Lounge is 19x16m and can comfortably sit up to 200 guests with an area for a dance floor. There is also a designated area for smokers just off the lounge.

The Riverview overlooks parkland, the Waikato River and the Hamilton Gardens making it a popular function room for all events.

You are welcome to come decorate the room to your liking providing no nails/tacks or tape are used on the wall or floor etc. We welcome all enquires regarding the hire of the Riverview.

Motorhome Park

We have a fully licensed Motorhome Park, however please note that we do not have a camp kitchen.

We have toilets and a coin-operated hot shower along with coin operated laundry facilities which are available 24 hours a day.

The cost is $18 per motorhome per night for a non-powered site, or $23 for a powered site. (Please note we have limited power sites available)

We are a pet friendly park as long as you clean up after your pets and dogs must be on a lead at all times. There is a dog exercise park close by where you can exercise your companion. There is also a dump station on site.

Please register at the main bar on arrival. It would be appreciated if you could time your arrival prior to 6.30pm.

You are also welcome to use the club facilities!
